Questions

FAQs

How far in advance should I book?
I typically book 2–4 weeks in advance, especially for weekend shoots. For corporate or extended sessions, 4–6 weeks is recommended. Reach out as early as possible to secure your preferred date.
What's your cancellation policy?
Full refunds are available if you cancel 48 hours or more before the session. Cancellations within 48 hours may be rescheduled once at no charge, subject to availability.
Do you travel for shoots?
Absolutely. I'm available for location shoots throughout the region. Travel fees may apply for locations more than 30 miles away — we'll discuss this during the booking process.
What format are the presets in?
Presets are delivered as both .DNG (for Lightroom Mobile) and .XMP (for Lightroom Classic and desktop). A quick-start PDF guide is included with every purchase.
Can I use the presets commercially?
Yes — presets come with a license for personal and commercial use. You can use them on client work, publications, and social media. Redistribution or resale of the preset files themselves is not permitted.
